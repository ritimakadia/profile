Hi Guys!

I’m a high school student with great ambitions for the future. Now, this might sound like every teenager but I aspire to do more than just “travel the world” or “become a billionaire”. I want to be a human. Something that may sound ridiculous but is indeed hard to find these days. People see life as a cycle and get sucked into it. Wake up, make money and repeat as new generations emerge. Rather than growing and bettering society, humans have somewhat “dehumanised” and seem to act like savages full of greed, grabbing every chance they have to get their hands on money. Yes, I have dreams to become a financial lawyer but I do not intend to be a part of the cycle. I wish to be human. Be kind to others, be ethical, be generous, work hard, tell the truth. Just follow a few simple morals to make life simple and stay human.
